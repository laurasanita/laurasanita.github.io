Short Bio

July 2003: Bachelor's degree in Management Engineering, from "Università di Roma Tor Vergata", Italy.
October 2005: Master's degree in Management Engineering, from "Università di Roma Tor Vergata", Italy.
January 2009: Doctoral degree in Operations Research, from "Università Sapienza di Roma", Rome, Italy.
February 2009 — August 2011: PostDoc at Discrete Optimization Group, EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland.
September 2011 — December 2011: World Trip (here is my route!)
January 2012 — June 2017: Assistant Professor at C&O, University of Waterloo, Canada.
July 2017 — January 2020: Associate Professor at C&O, University of Waterloo, Canada.
February 2020 — August 2022: Associate Professor at TU Eindhoven, Netherlands.
...since September 2022: Associate Professor at Bocconi University, Italy.
